About Lloyd Williams

After 30 years behind the wheel as an owner-operator, I’ve traded the full grind for freedom—but I’m not done driving. Not yet.

These days, I run select hauls on my own terms. No deadlines I don’t agree to. No dispatchers I don’t trust. I take the jobs I want, go where I want, and stay sharp doing the work I’ve always taken pride in—on time, no excuses, no hand-holding.

I built my name over decades: clean records, clean equipment, and clean handoffs. If I say I’ll be there, I will be. I don’t need the miles anymore. I just enjoy the road—and the people who still run it right. If we’ve worked together before, you know how I operate. If you haven’t—well, you’ll figure it out quick.
